Extra-Curricular Activities

Our school offers a range of extra-curricular activities designed to enhance students' skills and interests beyond the classroom. Here’s a glimpse of the exciting activities available:

Sports

Sports activities promote physical fitness, teamwork, and overall well-being. They help students develop discipline, perseverance, leadership skills, and a healthy lifestyle.

Excursions and Tours

Excursions and tours provide hands-on learning experiences outside the classroom. They broaden students' horizons and foster a deeper understanding of various subjects.

Art and Craft

Art and craft activities stimulate creativity and improve fine motor skills. They offer students a platform to express themselves and build confidence through artistic endeavors.

Music

Music activities enhance auditory skills and emotional expression. They contribute to cognitive development and help students develop patience and discipline through practice.

Dance

Dance activities promote physical fitness, coordination, and rhythm. They also enhance students' self-expression and boost confidence through performance.

Community Service

Community service activities help students develop a sense of social responsibility. They encourage empathy, teamwork, and active participation in societal betterment.
