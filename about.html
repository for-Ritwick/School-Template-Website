About Brainbotics International School

Brainbotics International School was founded in 2011 as a co-educational institution by Mr. Rajesh Verma, a prominent social activist. The school is dedicated to ensuring that its students reach their highest academic and personal potential. It also strives to provide a solid foundation based on an understanding of Indian culture, society, and values. Building on this foundation, the school aims to develop globally aware citizens equipped to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Brainbotics International School is thoughtfully designed to allow each student to engage in a wide variety of activities. This approach ensures that every student has the opportunity to discover and nurture their unique talents. Ultimately, our goal is to cultivate well-rounded individuals who not only excel academically but also grow into confident and innovative members of society.

Our Vision

Our vision at Brainbotics International School is to inspire every individual student to unlock their true potential through our outstanding education and make the world a better place.

Our teachers have an average of over 6 years’ experience and work to create a caring, supportive learning environment where children can fulfill their potential.

Our Mission

The mission of Brainbotics International School is to provide quality education through a holistic, scholastic, and spiritual approach, striving for excellence in all areas.

We ensure that our students develop skills in academics as well as co-curricular activities. We encourage our students to dream big and achieve their highest potential.
